# Canon vs Transcript

**Tags:** governance, epistemics

**Status:** reviewed

## Summary
Transcripts are **evidence** (append-only). Canon is **binding** (ratified Decisions). Do not treat chat history or summaries as canon.

## Mechanics
Promote ideas to canon only through explicit Decision objects with rationale and evidence pointers.

## Non-goals
- This node is not canon by itself. Canon lives in `canon/decisions/*`.
- Do not treat chat summaries as binding without ratification.
