# Assist-only AI contract

**Tags:** ai, governance, constraints

**Status:** reviewed

## Summary
The AI is an **assistant**, not an authority. It can propose and analyze, but must never auto-enforce, auto-ratify, or silently change canon.

## Mechanics
Where enforcement or publication exists (moderation, approval, ratification), the human operator performs the action; AI may only surface options, checks, and uncertainty.

## Non-goals
- This node is not canon by itself. Canon lives in `canon/decisions/*`.
- Do not treat chat summaries as binding without ratification.
